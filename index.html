FAQ

Good to know.

Is DownTo available now?

Not yet. The testing phase begins soon and a release date will be announced shortly after that.

What platforms will be supported?

DownTo is designed for iPhone and will be available on the Apple App Store for devices running iOS 26.0 or later.

Who sees my drops?

DownTo is built around your circle, so plans are shared with friends and no one else.

How is this app different from others?

DownTo is a lightweight social app that focuses on keeping plans organized and accessible without unnecessary features.

Is it free?

Yes! DownTo will be releasing as a completely free app with absolutely no in-app purchases or ads.

Will the app be updated and maintained?

Absolutely. Many features and improvements are already planned, as well as potential additional platform support over time.

Have additional questions?

Visit support to contact us for help, bug reports, feedback, or anything else.
