FAQ

Good to know.

Is DownTo available now?

Not yet, but you won't have to wait long. The testing phase is ongoing and a release date will be announced very soon.

What platforms will be supported?

DownTo is designed primarily for iPhone's running iOS 26.0 or later. It will also be available on iPadOS 26.0+ and macOS 26.0+.

Who sees my drops?

DownTo is built around your circle. Your friends are the only ones who can see your Moves, as there is no public feed.

How is this app different from others?

DownTo combines clear invitations, friend commitments, simple coordination, and accountability—without a public content feed.

Is it free?

Yes. DownTo is completely free to use, with no subscriptions, in-app purchases, or advertisements.

Will the app be updated and maintained?

Absolutely. Many features and improvements are already planned, as well as potential additional platform support over time.

Have additional questions?

Visit support to contact us for help, bug reports, feedback, or anything else.
